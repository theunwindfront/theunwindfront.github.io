Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Sagar Pansuriya ("we," "us," or "our") handles your information in relation to the mobile application Snake Reborn — Classic (the "App").

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This App is designed to be a private, offline-first experience.

Information Collection and Use

No Personal Information Collected: The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or location data.

Local Data Storage: The App tracks your game statistics and high scores. This data is stored locally on your device using a private database (SQLite). This data is used solely for the purpose of displaying your progress within the App and is not shared with us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics services, advertising SDKs, or social media integrations that track user behavior across apps or websites.

Children's Privacy

Because the App does not collect any personal information, it is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
